Jim Morrison

The Lizard King

James Douglas "Jim" Morrison was an American singer, songwriter, and poet, best remembered as the lead singer of The Doors.

Here's a time line of Jim Morrison life:

  • 1943 - Born in Melbourne, Florida, U.S.
  • 1971 - dies at the age of 27 in Paris, France.

Due to his lyrics, wild personality, performances, and the dramatic circumstances surrounding his life and death, Morrison is regarded by critics and fans as one of the most iconic and influential frontmen in rock music history.
